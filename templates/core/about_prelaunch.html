About FeedbackLoop

FeedbackLoop is a platform designed to help creators improve their projects through structured, actionable feedback. We believe in the power of peer feedback to elevate the quality of creative work.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a community where creators can exchange meaningful feedback in a fair and balanced way. We've designed FeedbackLoop with a credit-based system to ensure everyone both gives and receives quality feedback.

How It Works

FeedbackLoop operates on a simple principle: give feedback to get feedback. Users earn credits by providing thoughtful feedback on others' projects, which they can then spend to receive feedback on their own work.

Our Vision

We envision a world where creators at all levels have access to quality feedback that helps them improve their craft. Whether you're a student, freelancer, or professional, FeedbackLoop is designed to help you grow through structured peer review.
