About FeedbackLoop

Our Mission

FeedbackLoop is a community-driven platform designed to help creators improve their projects through thoughtful peer feedback. We believe that constructive criticism and diverse perspectives are essential for growth and innovation.

How It Works

Submit

Share your project with the community. Provide context and specific areas where you'd like feedback.

Exchange

Give feedback to others to earn credits. Use these credits to request feedback on your own projects.

Improve

Receive valuable insights from peers and use them to refine and enhance your work.

Our Values

  • Constructive Feedback: We encourage thoughtful, helpful criticism that focuses on improvement.
  • Community Support: Every creator deserves support and encouragement on their journey.
  • Fairness: Our credit system ensures everyone contributes to and benefits from the community.
  • Quality: We prioritize meaningful, detailed feedback over quantity.
  • Respect: All interactions should be respectful and considerate of others' efforts.
